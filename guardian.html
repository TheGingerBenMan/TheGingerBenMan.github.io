Crustacean Guardian

Crustacean Guardian was a brand study using Crustaceans as the basis. At the heart of the project, it came from a place of a running joke from my time early on in my design education which included crabs in different works of all kinds. While it came from a silly concept, it allowed me to explore and create a brand concept in a way that pushed me to think of odd concepts to fit this imaginary brand.
