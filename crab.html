Crustacean Guardian Brand

"Crustacean Guardian" is a mock brand I created in Intro to Graphic Design at the University of Massachusetts Dartmouth. The process begun with creating a brand idea and a logo, and then it expanded into an entire brand deck. The entire project was made over the course of a majority of the fall semester of 2022, going through a lot of changes along the way as I refined the brand.
