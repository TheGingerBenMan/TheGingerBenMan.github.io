Steam Deck Poster

The goal of the poster was to make something that celebrated a human made object. This could have been a bottle opener, baseball cap, or a light bulb. I decided on using Valve's Steam Deck as my subject, as I had got it recently at the time of the project. When I wasn't drawing or working on design projects, that would be one of the things I would decompress with. Beyond my interest in the object, it proved to be a nice challenge to render because of it's relatively complex design.
