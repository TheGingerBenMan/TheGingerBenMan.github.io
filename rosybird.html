Sir Bird of the Rosy Cheeks

"Sir Bird of the Rosy Cheeks" was created as a part of a pet portrait project in a photography and photoshop class at Massasoit Community College. At the time I did not have any pets, nor did I have access to good photos of any old pets. So I chose one of my favorite animals, a love Bird, and used photoshop to make it appear as a painted portrait. I spent a lot of time ironing out the details when working on it. I entered the poster in the Akillian Gallery art show at the school, and it ended up placing third.
