Tarot Cards

For my Digital Photography with Photoshop class at Massasoit Community College, we were tasked with making a few tarot cards about something we like. I chose to do mine on some games made by the company FROMSOFTWARE. We had to design a card back, and a couple different card fronts (such as a three of swords). I got really into the project and instead of doing three cards, I did 10. The class really helped me discover my love of photo editing and made me learn a lot about Photoshop as a program.
